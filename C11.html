Misha Collins is a Wonderful Human Being

A Short Essay

Misha Collins, born Dmitri Tippens Krushnic, is an American actor best known for his role as Castiel in the CW show, Supernatural. He has appeared as a guest star on multiple other shows including :

  • ER
  • CSI
  • NYPD Blue
  • 24
  • Monk
  • Charmed

He has experience in many field of work. He graduated University of Chicago with a degree in Social Theory. He had a job at NPR, was an intern in the White House during the Clinton, is a published poet, used carpentry to pay for college, and started his own software company.

But Misha Collins' talent and hardwork does not stop there. He is the founder of an organization called GISHWHES, or the Greatest International Scavenger Hunt the World Has Ever Seen. It is a program where people from around the world do crazy activities to spread kindness and positivity. It is partnered with Misha's other organization called Random Acts. Random Acts is a charity in which people donate money and perform random acts of kindness. One of the accomplishments of Random Acts is building a school in Nicaragua.

Misha Collins is a wonderful person. He is kind, compassionate, and talented. He uses his platform for good. I hope I can be as loving and caring as Misha, and have as much as an impact on the world.
